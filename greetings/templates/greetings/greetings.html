Welcome! In this section, you'll learn the most common Swahili greetings to help you connect and communicate warmly.

English Swahili Pronounciation
Hello / Hi Habari
I respect you (used when greeting elders) Shikamoo
I acknowledge your respect (response to "Shikamoo") Marahaba
What's up? Mambo?
Cool Poa (response to "Mambo")
How are you? (singular) Hujambo?
I’m fine (response to "Hujambo") Sijambo
How are you? (plural) Hamjambo?
We’re fine (response to "Hamjambo") Hatujambo
Fine Salama
Welcome Karibu
Thank you Asante
Goodbye (singular) Kwa heri
Goodbye (plural) Kwa herini
Good morning Habari ya asubuhi
Good afternoon Habari ya mchana
Good evening Habari ya jioni
Good night Usiku mwema
How’s your day? Habari za leo?
How’s your night? Habari za usiku?
